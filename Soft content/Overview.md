**Overview of the app to be built**

Basically a secretary for students, it will take in all the activities to be done for the day and with ai and algorithms be able to schedule your day for you. This is basically a personal secretary for anyone who uses the app.



**Features of the app**

* Remind (Duolingo-type-reminding) you of tasks to be done, assignments, deadlines
* To do list app, and automatically ticks when done
* Takes in all the schedules or timetables for your life
* Finds ongoing activities you might be interested in and then suggests them to you, based on your availability at that time.
* Advises on whether to take on some activities and how it will fit into your schedule.
* Alarm clock for waking
* Timing activities and suggests improvements
* Can edit importance allocated to various tasks
* See progress of how well-organized you've been for the day.
* Create your learning timetable
* Can register for activities for you, do bookings, find locations to places
* Basically another Jarvis (ironman)
* Help with assignments and projects powered with Ai
* Automate sending replies, responses.
* Setting constraints for task scheduling
* Timing of tasks (clock)
